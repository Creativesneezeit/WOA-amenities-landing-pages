Instructions for use:

  1. 01

    Get seated

    Sit comfortably in the PolarWave chair and make sure you are properly positioned before starting your session.

  2. 02

    Choose your settings

    Use the touchscreen to select your preferred session length and intensity level.

  3. 03

    Chill out

    Relax during the session while the controlled cold therapy helps support recovery and circulation.

  4. 04

    Finish slowly

    When your session is complete, exit slowly and allow your body a few moments to readjust.

Additional information

  • PolarWave delivers targeted cold therapy in a comfortable, controlled environment designed to help your body recover and recharge.
  • For newer users, it's recommended to start with shorter sessions and gradually work your way up as your body adjusts.
  • Stay hydrated before and after your session for the best experience.

Unlike traditional ice baths, it offers a more approachable recovery experience that can help reduce post-workout soreness, support circulation, and leave you feeling refreshed and energized.
