Instructions for use

  1. 01

    Reserve Your Bed Time

    Member signs log at the front desk for how long they want to lay, max 20 mins.

  2. 02

    Prepare for Your Session

    Member goes back to room and has a 3-minute timer to get into the bed once the time is set.

  3. 03

    Begin When Ready

    Member has the option to hit "Start" button to start bed early.

  4. 04

    Wipe Down the Bed

    Member cleans the bed with rag and cleaner supplied by the gym.

Additional information
